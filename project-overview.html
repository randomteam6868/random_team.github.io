Wellcome to our future project

Overview

Smart web-app for elderly

This project is about to improve the quality of lives of elders by its function. In this project, our object is the elders (60+ years old) who have many health problems. This project is a webapp that can do things that the user asks to do using a virtual assistant. The virtual assistant has a similar function with Siri, Bixby, and Google, but it is more advanced and good-looking for elders. Its function can be to measure health status, danger warnings, help people with hearing loss, assist, and conversation. It can also provide elders with some useful information for their health. If our project is successful, it will be a useful tool that can make the elders easier and more entertaining. When this project becomes successful, other developers can see its potential, inspiring them to finish all our ideas' function or improve it.

Our Motivation

This project gets inspiration from the fact that the population of our planet is getting older, this trend is especially relevant in developed Western countries. According to UNO estimations, the number of people aged 60+ will reach more than 2 billion people by 2050, which gives the seniors markets a huge opportunity for developers to jump in (Luvivity 2020).

However, the number of apps for this market is still very underdeveloped, while “nearly three quarters (74%) of 50-64 year-olds own a smartphone, while a little less than half (42%) of individuals who are 65 or older own a smartphone, according to Pew Research Center’s Mobile Fact Sheet, which is updated regularly throughout 2017” (Clutch.co 2020, para. 4).

My target is to step in this area and make an app that can inspire other developers to focus more on this market, thus improve the overall quality and performance of those apps. While the seniors are meeting with many problems such as loneliness, hearing loss, change in vision, etc. (Cdc.gov 2020), I want to somehow partly have a solution to help them with it.

Landscape

Many products are similar to our projects, such as Magnifying Glass with Light, skype, pillboxes, med coach, crosswords classic, Sudoku, luminosity, and red panic button. All this product can be our competitor in future. However, our project is more impressive than other products. Our product can do almost all these apps' functions, which is more convenient for the user. Each product in our list can only function, which requires the user to use many different products to use all the market functions.

Project's Aim

Our purpose of making this website is having profit from the elderly using our website and improve the elderly's life. We will find any brand that wants to put their advertisement on our website to have advertisement money. We also can charge our users by making registered money for our members to use our website. We can give them a week's trial, so it can attract them to use our website. Many functions on this website can help the elder's life more comfortable.

Measure health status

This function can gather the elderly’s heart rate and blood pressure data to help the elderly know their health status by showing on screen and through voice. If the senior does not understand by reading this information, they can choose virtual assistance to analyze and show them how to improve their health.

Danger warn

This function will call an ambulance or their relative or trusted contacts when the elders have an emergency such as having a stroke, a dizzy, and a fall.

Help people with hearing loss

The speech-to-text function can change the voice to the text that shows on the screen so they can read it. For example: if the senior wants to watch a video, our web will detect the video's voice and provide text, so the senior can understand the video.

Assist to blind people

Older people can use their voice to control their smartphone or access our website using a voice chatbot.

AI Assistant

Our chatbot function can help the elders set a reminder, look up information, and do a simple action on our website and the browser.

Making a community for elders

We can connect the user to chat with each other or make a status for the elderly.
