Skills & Jobs

As the project leaders, we will be the supervisor of the work progress and evaluate the efficiency, work speed, and performance of each team member.

The first person we would prefer to employ must be a web designer. To be more specific, that person will oversee building, designing, completing the website, and fixing it if something goes incorrectly. To acquire a designing website position, that person must be an expert on various web development platforms and languages for web designing. Furthermore, he/she also must have excellent communication skills since he/she is the one that gathers information from other members of our team and integrates them into the website system.

The second person should mention in our team the person in charge of building and developing the server, database, and how to connect parts of the web and connect users with the server. Since our project planned to use Nodejs and MySQL, the person I recruited would have the ability to program C/C++ at an advanced level. Also, he/she should be able to improvise and improve the server and database system when the project is put into operation. This ability required him/her to have critical thinking and anticipate situations that could happen when operating the project.

Another person who should be recruited is one who is a cybersecurity expert. In other words, he/she will monitor the website activities to check whether any unusual activities happen, protect the website from cyberattacks, and defend against the attacks. The larger a project growing, the higher the level of security experts will be required to modify and improve network security to suit the website's size.

The last person who should be mentioned in our team is marketing management. To be more specific, he/she will be responsible for finding out strategic plans to promote the website to consumers, researching the audience market to develop effective and appropriate web solutions and operations. We supposed that he/she must have experienced in marketing jobs, running ads, etc.
