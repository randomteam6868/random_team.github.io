What does this project do?

Elders who are over 65 years old take 8% of the world population in 2020, and it can be 16% in 2025(1). Lead to, the amount of health need of elders is increasing. However, the hospital may not receive all the elders, and it also cost the elders a lot. Therefore, we will use AI to make a web-app for the elderly, improving the elders' quality of life. This website can do many things based on its function, such as measuring health status, danger warnings, helping people with hearing loss, assisting, connecting to other people, and conversation. This website can show information about health status, danger warning when the elders have an emergency, assist for deaf and blind, and make a community for elders to reduce the loneliness.

Measure health status

It can measure heart rate, blood pressure, and vision using the hardware and software available on their device or base on the data user provide to our website through AI chatbot. This function also takes note of every information related to the elders' health and store in cloud computing to follow the elders' health day by day. If they need an advisory for their health, our AI software can analyze their health status to give them an excellent advisory to their health. Thus, the senior does not have to go to the hospital and check their health every day. There is a storing blood group function we want to do so on, which can be used in an emergency. For instance, when the elders have emergency surgery, the doctor can access our cloud computing to know which group of blood they are. It can reduce the time to check their blood group and take actions immediately in emergency situations.

Danger warn

This function is one of the most important features of our website because it can save the elders' lives. In old age, there is some diseases that we cannot check, and it can appear at any time that can kill the elders live such as stroke and heart attack. So, we have decided to make a function that may save their lives by sending an emergency message to their relative or hospital. For example, suppose an elderly have a heart attack. In that case, our AI will measure the senior response in the chatbot to know elder issue and sending an emergency message to the hospital and relative to they can send an ambulance. We also want to add a function that can make noise to attract people to come to help.

Help blind and deaf senior

This function is useful to blind and deaf people. For deaf people, there is a function that can convert the voice into text using virtual assistants, so they can enjoy a movie or a news video. For blind people, the senior can control the website by using their voice command. So, the chatbot can do some action on the website like converting the news from text to voice.

Making community for elders

This function propose is to communicate with other people. In old age, many elders feel lonely because they do not have someone to talk to or make friends with, so we decide to make a social network to gather elders in different places to talk. This social network will be like Facebook, but it is only available for elders to make friends or share their status in that network. It can make them happier in their lives because the elders' lifestyle is boring. In this community, they can share useful information.
